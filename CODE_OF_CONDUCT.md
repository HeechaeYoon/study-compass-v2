# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We aim to keep this project welcoming, student-centered, and respectful. Contributors should communicate in ways that are considerate of teachers, students, maintainers, and other contributors.

## Expected behavior

- Use inclusive and respectful language.
- Focus critique on the code, product behavior, documentation, or evidence.
- Be especially careful with wording that could stigmatize students or imply fixed ability.
- Protect student privacy and never share real student data.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about product scope, safety language, and classroom constraints.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or student data.
- Encouraging collection or transmission of student data outside the local browser.
- Repeatedly pushing fixed-personality, ranking, or deficit-based language after maintainers reject it.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may close issues or pull requests that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may lead to blocking from the repository.
